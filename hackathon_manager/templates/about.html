About Hackathon Management

Hackathon Management is a web application designed to help you manage your hackathon event. Whether you're planning a small local hackathon or a large international event, Hackathon Management has the tools and resources you need to make it a success.

Features

With Hackathon Management, you can:

  • Create and manage hackathon events, including event details, dates, location, and registration information
  • Register and manage participants, including team registration and member management
  • Organize and manage teams, including team creation, management, and communication
  • Track progress and milestones, including deadlines, checkpoints, and judging criteria
  • Manage sponsors and prizes, including sponsor registration and prize management

Benefits

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and customizable, so you can create a unique experience for your hackathon participants. Some of the benefits of using Hackathon Management include:

  • Streamlined organization and management, allowing you to focus on creating a great hackathon experience
  • Automated registration and team creation, making it easy for participants to sign up and join teams
  • Easy communication and collaboration, facilitating teamwork and idea sharing
  • Real-time progress tracking, allowing you to stay on top of key milestones and deadlines
  • Seamless sponsor and prize management, making it easy to attract and reward top talent

Customization

We understand that every hackathon is unique, which is why we've made Hackathon Management fully customizable. You can easily modify the platform to fit your specific needs, including:

  • Customizing the event registration form to collect the information you need
  • Creating custom fields for participant and team profiles
  • Setting up custom judging criteria and scoring rubrics
  • Customizing the design and branding of your hackathon website

Support

  • 24/7 email and chat support
  • Video tutorials and documentation to help you get started
  • Regular updates and new feature releases
